About Us

WHAT?

PocketSomm is an artificial intelligence sommelier.Currently, when you want to pair wine and food you have five options:

  • Asking a waiter
  • Asking a sommelier
  • Asking in the store
  • Reading off the bottle
  • Studying yourself (takes more time and effort than creating this AI)

None of these methods fit the modern need for instant and accurate information. We are here to change that: PocketSomm recommends a number of wines that match perfectly what you are about to eat or cook. We would even venture as far as to say that our artificial intelligence, in comparison with an actual sommelier, is better. It has more memory, processing power and best of all: goes anywhere with you.
